Our designs make the ordinary EXTRAORDINARY

I am a simple housewife but designing was always on my mind. One day I was sitting with a couple of friends in a cafe when they asked me to help them get their tops stitched. To begin with I was not prepared at all but soon I started taking guidance in the working and the materials. In the beginning there were a few hiccups but I kept on moving passionately over five years and soon there was no turning back. I started this all with a room but with God's grace and my efforts I have now made a studio for my venture, QUEENZ CLOSET
